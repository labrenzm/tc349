My process for this was as follows: Every category and sub-category item had an index card. I asked my friends to group items into categories, with one card forming the category head. The data from these 2 that differed the most led me to a couple conclusions about what I should think about more as I design my site.

  • First, that the 2012 and 2013 items can be fairly confusing as to where they belong. In my mind, I had them going into the archives section, listing every post that had the 2012 or 2013 tag on them. One person had the same thought, and the other related it to the projects that I may have completed in 2012 or 2013.
  • Secondly, that the current happenings probably shouldn't be included as a link for my site. Almost all 4 sorts had it in a different spot.
